🏫   About ConnectUs

The Full Picture.
For Every Student.

ConnectUs replaces scattered spreadsheets and paper grade books with one connected platform — giving schools, teachers, students, and parents a complete, real-time view of academic performance.

The Problem

Schools Still Run on Spreadsheets

Walk into most schools today and you'll find the same thing — teachers juggling dozens of Excel sheets, grade books filled with hand-written entries, and report cards typed out one by one at the end of term.

It's slow. It's error-prone. And worst of all, nobody has the full picture. Parents don't know how their child is doing until a report card arrives weeks later. Students don't know where they stand until it's too late to improve.

By the time anyone finds out there's a problem, the term is already over.

The Solution

One Platform. Everyone Connected.

ConnectUs brings the entire school community onto one shared, real-time platform. The moment a teacher enters a grade, everyone who needs to see it can see it.

🔑

School Admins

See a live dashboard of school-wide academic performance. Manage teachers, students, and classes from one place. Generate report cards with a single click. Know exactly how the school is performing — at any moment.

🎓

Teachers

Enter grades directly into a live gradebook — no spreadsheets, no manual calculations. Manage your class roster, leave notes on assignments, run evaluations, and generate professional report cards in minutes.

👪

Students & Parents

Log in from any device and see grades as soon as they're entered. View the full academic transcript, check progress across subjects, and know exactly what areas need improvement — before it's too late.

⚡

Real-Time. Not End-of-Term.

The biggest shift ConnectUs makes is simple: nobody is waiting anymore. A teacher enters a grade on Monday. A parent checks it on Tuesday. A student knows where they stand by Wednesday. The school sees the full picture every single day — not just at the end of term when it's too late to do anything about it.

Our Vision

No Student's Record
Should Ever Be Lost

Every student who goes through ConnectUs carries a permanent Academic Passport — a lifelong digital record of their academic journey. Every teacher carries a Career Ledger that follows them throughout their professional life.

When a student moves to a new school, their history moves with them. When a teacher joins a new institution, their record is already there. No paperwork. No starting over. No lost years.
